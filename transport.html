Transport facilities

         The school has partnered with a safe and secure bus and van transport facility to pick-up and drop all day-scholars at their scheduled stop. Parents are expected to bring their child/ward to the bus stop as notified and escort them back from the same stop.

         For more information on routes and timings please contact the school administration office.
